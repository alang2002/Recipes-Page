Cake

Recipe adapted from here

Jump back to the top

Ingredients Directions
2 cups granulated sugar 1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
1/2 cup vegetable oil 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, vegetable oil, eggs and vanilla extract. Beat at medium speed until the eggs and oil are well mixed in and the batter is lighter in color, around 2 minutes.
3 large eggs 3. Mix in the sour cream and beat until well mixed.
1 tablespoon vanilla extract 4. Add half the flour, followed by the baking powder and salt. Beat on low speed while pouring in half of the milk. Mix just until the flour starts to integrate.
1/2 cup light sour cream 5. Finally add the remaining flour, mixing on low speed while pouring in the remaining milk and beating until all of the ingredients are well combined.
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour 6. Scrape down the sides with a spatula and stir the batter from bottom to top to ensure it's well mixed.
3 tablespoons baking powder 7. Pour the batter in a 9 inch by 13 inch pan, and bake at 350F for 28-32 minutes. Keep a careful eye on the baking during the last few minutes. Remove from oven when complete, and allow to cool completely.
1 teaspoon salt 8. Test the cake for doneness by inserting a toothpick into the center of the cake. If it comes out clean, your cake is done.
1 1/4 cups milk 9. Spread frosting as desired. Serve warm, and refrigerate leftovers.
